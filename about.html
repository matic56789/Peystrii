About Us

Our Story

Our pastry store has a rich history that dates back to the early 1900s, started as a small bakery on the streets of Manila, and quickly gained a reputation for his delicious and beautiful pastries.

Over the years, the bakery changed hands several times, but the tradition of high-quality and delicious pastries remained. In the early 2000s, the bakery underwent a transformation. We introduced new flavors and techniques while also preserving the bakery's traditional recipes and methods. The result was a bakery that combined the best of traditional and modern pastry-making.

Today, our pastry store continues to build on this legacy of high-quality and delicious pastries. We are committed to using only the finest ingredients and techniques to create pastries that are not only delicious, but also visually stunning. Our team of skilled bakers and pastry chefs are dedicated to crafting each pastry with care and attention to detail, and we are proud to continue the tradition of high-quality pastry-making that has been part of our bakery's history for over a century.

Mission & Vision

Mission

Our mission is to create delicious, high-quality pastries that are made with the finest ingredients and crafted with care and attention to detail. We strive to provide our customers with a welcoming, warm and cozy atmosphere where they can enjoy our pastries along with their favorite coffee or tea. We are committed to delivering exceptional customer service and building long-lasting relationships with our customers.

Vision

Our vision is to become the go-to destination for pastry lovers in our community. We aim to be recognized for our commitment to excellence in baking, our passion for creativity and innovation in our recipes, and our dedication to providing outstanding customer service. We envision a future where our pastry shop is a place where people come together to indulge in their favorite treats, connect with each other, and create happy memories.

Support us

At Paistrii, we are committed to giving back to the community that has supported us. We believe in using our resources and expertise to help those in need, particularly in the areas of hunger relief and education.
